Create a charcoal drawing of a lonely man walking in the rain at night under a streetlight. The artwork should focus on line, value, contrast, texture, and movement. Use vine charcoal and compressed charcoal on paper to build dark shadows and soft gray tones. Use smudging and layering to make the night look foggy and quiet. Add light cross-hatching to create deeper shadows. Show the rain with simple diagonal lines to give a sense of movement. Use an eraser to make small highlights on the wet ground to show reflection from the streetlight. The overall feeling should be calm, sad, and lonely.
